# A Survey of Reinforcement Learning for Large Reasoning Models

**Section:** Survey and Overview (additional)

**Paper:** [arXiv:2509.08827](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.08827)

**Venue:** arXiv, October 2025

---

## Why This Paper Is Relevant

Academic survey covering RL for Large Reasoning Models (LRMs) post-DeepSeek-R1. Useful for **understanding the broader context** and ensuring our related work section is comprehensive.

---

## Key Content

Covers the academic landscape of RL applied to reasoning models:
- Post-DeepSeek-R1 developments
- Taxonomy of RL methods for reasoning
- Comparison of approaches (RLHF, RLVR, GRPO, and variants)
- Open problems and future directions

---

## How We Use It in Distributional GEPA

1. **Related work completeness:** Cite for comprehensive coverage of the RL landscape. Ensures we don't miss relevant RL approaches in our positioning.

2. **Reviewer expectations:** EMNLP reviewers may expect awareness of the RL-for-reasoning landscape. This survey helps us demonstrate that awareness.

3. **Framing GEPA's niche:** The survey highlights what RL methods can and cannot do — helping us position GEPA's prompt-only approach as complementary rather than competing.
